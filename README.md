# NearHai – Hyperlocal Interaction Platform

## Overview

**NearHai** is a hyperlocal digital platform that connects people with their nearby local world. The goal of NearHai is to make it easier to discover nearby shops, services, transport, events, and local information through an interactive map-based interface.

Many useful services exist physically around us but are difficult to discover digitally. NearHai solves this by creating a **real-time local interaction layer** that connects users, businesses, and communities.

The platform integrates **local discovery, delivery, transport, services, and social interaction** into one application.

---

# Problem

In many towns and cities, a large number of **local businesses and services are not properly visible online**. Because of this:

- People often **roam around markets** searching for products.
- Small shops **lack digital presence**.
- Platforms like Google Maps or Justdial mostly provide **static listings**.
- Local transport systems like **shared autos wait until seats are full**, wasting time.
- Affordable **hyperlocal delivery systems are limited**.

This creates a gap between the **physical local world and the digital ecosystem**.

---

# Solution

NearHai bridges this gap through a **map-based hyperlocal interaction platform**.

Users can discover nearby:

- Shops  
- Services  
- Transport  
- Events  
- Emergency facilities  

Businesses and service providers can list their offerings and interact directly with nearby users.

NearHai also introduces innovative systems like **Intent Mode, Routine Delivery, and Karma Rewards** to improve efficiency and engagement.

---

# Core Features

## 1. Intent Mode System

NearHai introduces an **Intent Mode interface** that changes the map according to user needs.

Modes include:

- Buy
- Travel
- Services
- Explore
- Emergency

This allows users to quickly switch between different types of local interactions.

---

## 2. Hyperlocal Map Discovery

Users can view nearby services on a real-time map.

Examples:

- Nearby electronics shop
- Nearby auto rickshaw
- Nearby repair technician
- Nearby events or markets

This removes the need to manually search across multiple platforms.

---

## 3. Instant Delivery

Users can request **fast delivery from nearby shops**.

Features include:

- Quick dispatch
- Real-time tracking
- Priority delivery

---

## 4. Routine Delivery System

NearHai introduces a **scheduled batch delivery system**.

Delivery happens in fixed time slots:

- 9 AM
- 12 PM
- 3 PM
- 6 PM
- 9 PM

A delivery agent can deliver multiple orders in a single trip.

Benefits:

- Lower delivery cost
- Reduced fuel consumption
- Improved efficiency

Routine delivery is **cheaper than instant delivery**.

---

## 5. Karma Reward System

NearHai includes a **gamified reward system** called Karma.

Users and businesses earn points for activities such as:

- Placing orders
- Providing services
- Posting updates
- Helping the community

Karma points can be redeemed for:

- Discounts
- Free delivery
- Promotions
- Advertisement boosts

This encourages active participation within the platform.

---

## 6. Hyperlocal Social Media Feed

NearHai also functions as a **local community platform**.

The app includes a **Twitter-style feed** where:

- Local journalists can post news
- Residents can share updates
- People can ask local questions
- Community events can be announced

This helps create a **digitally connected local community**.
